## For Candidates

### Before the Interview

#### 1. **Prepare Your Environment**
- Ensure good lighting and clear visibility of your face
- Use a quiet, professional background
- Test your internet connection
- Close unnecessary applications
- Have a notepad nearby (physical, not digital)

#### 2. **Technical Requirements**
- Webcam must be functional and visible
- Microphone (if audio/video mode)
- Stable internet connection
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- No VPN or proxy services

#### 3. **Identity Verification**
- The system will capture your photo for identity verification
- This ensures interview integrity and fairness
- Periodic verification snapshots will be taken during the interview
- Your face must be clearly visible throughout

### During the Interview

#### 1. **Interview Modes**

**Text Mode**
- Type your answers directly
- No time limit per question (but overall session timer applies)
- Best for: Technical questions, detailed explanations
- Tips: Be specific, provide examples, explain your reasoning

**Audio Mode**
- Speak your answers (transcribed in real-time)
- Measures: Speech rate, fluency, confidence
- Best for: Communication skills assessment
- Tips: Speak clearly, maintain professional tone, avoid filler words

**Video Mode**
- Full video recording with audio
- Measures: Body language, eye contact, professionalism
- Best for: Comprehensive behavioral assessment
- Tips: Maintain eye contact with camera, professional appearance, clear speech

#### 2. **Answering Questions**

**Quality Guidelines:**
- Answer directly and concisely
- Provide specific examples from your experience
- Explain your reasoning and thought process
- Be honest - the system detects AI-generated content
- Use your own words and knowledge

**What NOT to Do:**
- ❌ Copy-paste from external sources (disabled and flagged)
- ❌ Use AI tools to generate answers
- ❌ Switch browser tabs or windows
- ❌ Leave the interview window
- ❌ Use external resources or notes
- ❌ Have someone else answer for you

#### 3. **Monitoring & Integrity**

The system actively monitors:
- **Tab Switches**: Switching away from the interview is tracked
- **Copy-Paste Attempts**: Disabled and flagged if attempted
- **AI Content Detection**: Analyzes answers for AI-generated content
- **Behavioral Patterns**: Speech rate, confidence, consistency
- **Identity Verification**: Periodic face verification during interview

**Integrity Warnings:**
- Multiple violations may result in interview termination
- Flagged content will be reviewed by employers
- Severe violations may disqualify your application

#### 4. **Time Management**

- Each interview has a total time limit (typically 60 minutes)
- Timer is displayed in the top-right corner
- When time expires, your current answer is auto-submitted
- Manage your time across all questions
- Aim for 3-5 minutes per question

### After the Interview

#### 1. **Interview Report**
- Automatically generated within 24 hours
- Includes AI evaluation and employer feedback
- Shows your scores across multiple dimensions
- Available in your candidate dashboard

#### 2. **Scores Explained**

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Technical Score** | Depth of technical knowledge and problem-solving |
| **Communication Score** | Clarity, articulation, and explanation ability |
| **Problem-Solving Score** | Approach to challenges, creativity, logic |
| **Soft Skills Score** | Professionalism, attitude, cultural fit |
| **Confidence Score** | Certainty and conviction in answers |
| **Fluency Score** | Speech smoothness and coherence (audio/video) |
| **Professionalism Score** | Overall professional demeanor |
| **Overall Score** | Weighted average of all metrics |

#### 3. **Next Steps**
- Employer reviews your interview report
- You'll receive feedback or next interview invitation
- Check your email for updates

## For Employers

### Creating an Interview

#### 1. **Job Setup**
- Create a job posting with detailed requirements
- Specify required skills and experience level
- Define interview strictness level:
  - **Lenient**: Easier questions, more guidance
  - **Moderate**: Balanced difficulty (recommended)
  - **Strict**: Challenging questions, minimal guidance

#### 2. **Interview Configuration**

**Select Interview Mode:**
- **Text**: Best for technical roles, detailed assessment
- **Audio**: Best for communication-heavy roles
- **Video**: Best for client-facing, leadership roles

**Set Parameters:**
- Number of questions (typically 8-12)
- Time limit per interview (typically 45-60 minutes)
- Question types: Technical, Behavioral, Problem-solving
- Difficulty levels: Junior, Mid-level, Senior

#### 3. **AI Question Generation**

The system automatically generates questions based on:
- Job requirements and skills
- Experience level
- Industry best practices
- Strictness level selected

**Question Types:**
- **Technical**: Assess job-specific knowledge
- **Behavioral**: Assess soft skills and experience
- **Problem-Solving**: Assess analytical thinking
- **Follow-up**: Dynamically generated based on answers

### Reviewing Interviews

#### 1. **Interview Dashboard**
- View all candidates' interviews for a job
- Filter by status: In Progress, Completed, Reviewed
- Sort by score, date, or candidate name

#### 2. **Candidate Report**

**Key Sections:**
- **Overall Score**: Weighted average (0-100)
- **Technical Assessment**: Knowledge and skills evaluation
- **Communication Analysis**: Clarity and articulation
- **Behavioral Insights**: Soft skills and cultural fit
- **Integrity Report**: Anti-cheat monitoring results
- **AI Evaluation**: Detailed feedback on each answer

#### 3. **Integrity Monitoring**

**Anti-Cheat Metrics:**
- Tab switches detected
- Copy-paste attempts
- AI-generated content flags
- Identity verification status
- Behavioral anomalies

**Risk Levels:**
- 🟢 **Low**: No suspicious activity
- 🟡 **Medium**: Minor violations, review recommended
- 🔴 **High**: Multiple violations, manual review required

#### 4. **Making Decisions**

**Review Process:**
1. Check overall score and technical assessment
2. Review AI evaluation and feedback
3. Check integrity report for any flags
4. Read candidate's actual answers
5. Consider communication and soft skills
6. Make hiring decision

**Decision Options:**
- ✅ **Accept**: Move to next round or offer
- ⏸️ **Hold**: Review later or request additional info
- ❌ **Reject**: Provide feedback to candidate

#### 5. **Providing Feedback**

**Best Practices:**
- Be specific about strengths and weaknesses
- Reference actual answers and examples
- Provide constructive suggestions
- Be professional and respectful
- Explain your decision clearly

## Evaluation Metrics

### Technical Score (0-100)
**Measures:** Job-specific knowledge, technical depth, problem-solving approach

**Scoring Breakdown:**
- 90-100: Expert level, exceptional knowledge
- 80-89: Advanced level, strong understanding
- 70-79: Proficient level, good knowledge
- 60-69: Competent level, adequate knowledge
- Below 60: Needs improvement

### Communication Score (0-100)
**Measures:** Clarity, articulation, explanation ability, professionalism

**Scoring Breakdown:**
- 90-100: Excellent communicator, very clear
- 80-89: Good communicator, mostly clear
- 70-79: Adequate communicator, generally clear
- 60-69: Fair communicator, somewhat unclear
- Below 60: Poor communication

### Problem-Solving Score (0-100)
**Measures:** Analytical thinking, approach to challenges, creativity, logic

**Scoring Breakdown:**
- 90-100: Exceptional problem-solver
- 80-89: Strong problem-solver
- 70-79: Good problem-solver
- 60-69: Adequate problem-solver
- Below 60: Weak problem-solver

### Soft Skills Score (0-100)
**Measures:** Professionalism, attitude, cultural fit, teamwork indicators

**Scoring Breakdown:**
- 90-100: Excellent soft skills
- 80-89: Strong soft skills
- 70-79: Good soft skills
- 60-69: Adequate soft skills
- Below 60: Needs improvement

### Confidence Score (0-100)
**Measures:** Certainty in answers, conviction, decisiveness

**Scoring Breakdown:**
- 90-100: Very confident, decisive
- 80-89: Confident, mostly decisive
- 70-79: Reasonably confident
- 60-69: Somewhat uncertain
- Below 60: Lacks confidence

### Overall Score (0-100)
**Calculation:** Weighted average of all metrics

**Typical Weights:**
- Technical: 40%
- Communication: 20%
- Problem-Solving: 20%
- Soft Skills: 10%
- Confidence: 10%

**Interpretation:**
- 90-100: Excellent candidate, strong hire
- 80-89: Very good candidate, good hire
- 70-79: Good candidate, consider hiring
- 60-69: Adequate candidate, needs further evaluation
- Below 60: Weak candidate, likely not suitable

## Best Practices

### For Candidates

#### Before Interview
✅ **Do:**
- Test all technical requirements
- Prepare your environment
- Review job description
- Think about relevant examples
- Get good sleep
- Arrive 10 minutes early

❌ **Don't:**
- Use AI tools to prepare answers
- Memorize scripted responses
- Attempt to cheat the system
- Use external resources during interview
- Have distractions nearby

#### During Interview
✅ **Do:**
- Answer honestly and thoughtfully
- Provide specific examples
- Explain your reasoning
- Ask for clarification if needed
- Maintain professional demeanor
- Speak clearly and confidently

❌ **Don't:**
- Copy-paste or use external sources
- Switch tabs or windows
- Rush through answers
- Use filler words excessively
- Appear distracted or disengaged
- Attempt to deceive the system

#### After Interview
✅ **Do:**
- Review your performance
- Note areas for improvement
- Follow up professionally
- Thank the employer
- Prepare for next round

❌ **Don't:**
- Contact employer repeatedly
- Make excuses for poor performance
- Attempt to change answers
- Complain about the system

### For Employers

#### Before Interview
✅ **Do:**
- Define clear job requirements
- Set appropriate difficulty level
- Communicate expectations to candidates
- Prepare evaluation criteria
- Brief your team on process

❌ **Don't:**
- Ask discriminatory questions
- Set unrealistic expectations
- Bias the question generation
- Skip integrity checks

#### During Interview
✅ **Do:**
- Monitor for integrity issues
- Take notes on candidate performance
- Review actual answers carefully
- Consider context and circumstances
- Be fair and objective

❌ **Don't:**
- Interrupt or distract candidates
- Make snap judgments
- Ignore integrity warnings
- Discriminate based on protected characteristics

#### After Interview
✅ **Do:**
- Review comprehensive report
- Consider all metrics
- Provide constructive feedback
- Make timely decisions
- Communicate clearly with candidates

❌ **Don't:**
- Make decisions based on single metric
- Ignore integrity concerns
- Delay feedback unnecessarily
- Provide vague rejection reasons

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"Webcam not detected"**
- Check browser permissions
- Restart browser
- Try different browser
- Check device settings

**"Copy-paste is disabled"**
- This is intentional for integrity
- Type your answer instead
- Use physical notes if needed

**"AI content detected"**
- Ensure you're using your own words
- Avoid paraphrasing AI tools
- Write naturally and authentically

**"Interview timed out"**
- Manage time better across questions
- Practice time management
- Don't spend too long on one question

**"Low integrity score"**
- Avoid tab switching
- Don't attempt copy-paste
- Maintain consistent behavior
- Keep face visible
