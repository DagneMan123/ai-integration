# Interview Quick Reference Card

## 🎯 Interview Modes at a Glance

| Mode | Best For | Key Metrics | Duration |
|------|----------|-------------|----------|
| **Text** | Technical roles | Knowledge, Problem-solving | 45-60 min |
| **Audio** | Communication roles | Fluency, Confidence, Tone | 45-60 min |
| **Video** | Leadership roles | Presence, Body language, Professionalism | 45-60 min |

---

## ✅ Candidate Checklist

### Before Interview
- [ ] Test webcam and microphone
- [ ] Check internet connection
- [ ] Prepare quiet, professional space
- [ ] Close unnecessary applications
- [ ] Have notepad nearby (physical)
- [ ] Review job description
- [ ] Prepare relevant examples

### During Interview
- [ ] Keep face visible to camera
- [ ] Answer honestly and thoughtfully
- [ ] Provide specific examples
- [ ] Explain your reasoning
- [ ] Manage time across questions
- [ ] Maintain professional demeanor
- [ ] Speak clearly and confidently

### After Interview
- [ ] Review your performance
- [ ] Wait for employer feedback
- [ ] Follow up professionally
- [ ] Prepare for next round

---

## 🚫 What NOT to Do

| ❌ Don't | Why | Consequence |
|---------|-----|-------------|
| Use AI tools | Detected automatically | Disqualification |
| Copy-paste | Disabled and flagged | Integrity warning |
| Switch tabs | Monitored and tracked | Integrity score reduced |
| Leave window | Tracked as suspicious | Integrity warning |
| Use external resources | Violates integrity | Disqualification |
| Have someone else answer | Identity verification fails | Disqualification |

## 📊 Scoring Breakdown

### Overall Score Interpretation
- **90-100**: Excellent candidate ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **80-89**: Very good candidate ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **70-79**: Good candidate ⭐⭐⭐
- **60-69**: Adequate candidate ⭐⭐
- **Below 60**: Weak candidate ⭐

### Score Components
- **Technical (40%)**: Job-specific knowledge
- **Communication (20%)**: Clarity and articulation
- **Problem-Solving (20%)**: Analytical thinking
- **Soft Skills (10%)**: Professionalism and attitude
- **Confidence (10%)**: Certainty and decisiveness

## 🛡️ Integrity Monitoring

### What's Tracked
✓ Tab switches  
✓ Copy-paste attempts  
✓ AI-generated content  
✓ Identity verification  
✓ Behavioral patterns  

### Risk Levels
- 🟢 **Low**: No suspicious activity
- 🟡 **Medium**: Minor violations
- 🔴 **High**: Multiple violations

---

## 👔 Employer Decision Guide

### Review Process
1. Check overall score
2. Review technical assessment
3. Check integrity report
4. Read actual answers
5. Consider soft skills
6. Make decision

### Decision Options
- ✅ **Accept**: Move forward
- ⏸️ **Hold**: Review later
- ❌ **Reject**: Provide feedback

---

## ⏱️ Time Management Tips

- **Total Time**: 45-60 minutes
- **Per Question**: 3-5 minutes
- **Buffer**: 5-10 minutes for review
- **Strategy**: Don't spend too long on one question

---

## 🎤 Audio/Video Tips

### Do's
✓ Speak clearly and confidently  
✓ Maintain eye contact with camera  
✓ Professional appearance  
✓ Avoid filler words ("um", "uh")  
✓ Maintain steady pace  

### Don'ts
✗ Mumble or speak too fast  
✗ Look away from camera  
✗ Casual appearance  
✗ Excessive filler words  
✗ Inconsistent pace  

---

## 📝 Text Mode Tips

### Do's
✓ Type complete sentences  
✓ Provide specific examples  
✓ Explain your reasoning  
✓ Proofread before submitting  
✓ Be thorough but concise  

### Don'ts
✗ Use abbreviations excessively  
✗ Provide vague answers  
✗ Skip explanations  
✗ Submit without reviewing  
✗ Write too much

## 🔍 AI Content Detection

### What Gets Flagged
- ChatGPT-style responses
- Plagiarized content
- Copy-pasted material
- Suspicious patterns
- Inconsistent writing style

### How to Avoid
- Use your own words
- Write naturally
- Provide personal examples
- Show your thinking process
- Be authentic

## 🎯 Success Tips

### For Candidates
1. **Prepare thoroughly** - Know the job requirements
2. **Be authentic** - Use your own words and knowledge
3. **Provide examples** - Back up claims with real experiences
4. **Manage time** - Don't rush, but stay on schedule
5. **Stay professional** - Maintain professional demeanor throughout
6. **Follow guidelines** - Respect integrity requirements
7. **Review answers** - Proofread before submitting

### For Employers
1. **Set clear expectations** - Define job requirements clearly
2. **Choose right mode** - Select interview mode matching role
3. **Review thoroughly** - Don't rely on score alone
4. **Check integrity** - Review anti-cheat data
5. **Consider context** - Understand candidate circumstances
6. **Provide feedback** - Help candidates improve
7. **Make timely decisions** - Don't delay unnecessarily

## 📋 Interview Strictness Levels

### Lenient
- Easier questions
- More guidance provided
- Lower difficulty expectations
- Best for: Entry-level positions

### Moderate (Recommended)
- Balanced difficulty
- Standard expectations
- Fair assessment
- Best for: Mid-level positions

### Strict
- Challenging questions
- Minimal guidance
- High difficulty expectations
- Best for: Senior/expert positions

## 🚨 Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Webcam not detected | Check browser permissions, restart browser |
| Copy-paste disabled | Type answer instead, use physical notes |
| AI content flagged | Use your own words, write naturally |
| Interview timed out | Manage time better, practice pacing |
| Low integrity score | Avoid tab switching, stay focused |
| Technical difficulties | Contact support, try different browser |
