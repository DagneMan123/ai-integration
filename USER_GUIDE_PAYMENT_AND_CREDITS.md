# User Guide: Payment & Credits System

## Overview
SimuAI uses a credit-based system for interviews. You need credits to start interviews.

## Credit System

### What is a Credit?
- 1 Credit = 5 ETB (Ethiopian Birr)
- Each interview costs 1 credit
- Credits are purchased in bundles

### Available Bundles
| Bundle | Credits | Price (ETB) | Price per Credit |
|--------|---------|------------|-----------------|
| Small | 20 | 100 | 5 ETB |
| Medium | 50 | 250 | 5 ETB |
| Large | 100 | 500 | 5 ETB |
| Extra Large | 200 | 1000 | 5 ETB |

### Example
- Buy 100 Credits for 500 ETB
- Start 100 interviews (1 credit each)
- Each interview costs 5 ETB

---

## How to Purchase Credits

### Step 1: Go to Payments
1. Login to your account
2. Click on "Payments" or "Billing" in the sidebar
3. You'll see available credit bundles

### Step 2: Select Bundle
1. Choose the bundle you want
2. Click "Buy Now" or "Purchase"
3. Review the amount (in ETB)

### Step 3: Complete Payment
1. You'll be redirected to Chapa payment gateway
2. Enter your payment details
3. Complete the payment
4. You'll see "Payment Confirmed!" message

### Step 4: Credits Added
1. Your wallet balance will update
2. You can now start interviews
3. Check your balance in the dashboard

---

## How to Start an Interview

### Prerequisites
- ✅ You must have at least 1 credit
- ✅ You must have applied for a job
- ✅ The job must be active

### Steps
1. Go to "Jobs" page
2. Find a job you applied for
3. Click "Start Interview"
4. Interview will begin
5. 1 credit will be deducted from your wallet

### During Interview
- Answer all questions
- Submit your responses
- Complete the interview
- View your report

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: "Insufficient credits. Please top up 5 ETB."

**Meaning**: You don't have enough credits to start an interview.

**Solution**:
1. Go to Payments page
2. Purchase a credit bundle
3. Complete payment
4. Try starting interview again

### Issue: "You have already applied for this job"

**Meaning**: You've already applied for this job and can't apply again.

**Solution**:
- You can only apply once per job
- If you want to interview again, wait for the employer to post a new job
- Or apply for a different job

### Issue: "Payment Confirmed!" but no credits added

**Meaning**: Payment was successful but credits haven't been added yet.

**Solution**:
1. Wait 30 seconds
2. Refresh the page
3. Check your wallet balance
4. If still not added, contact support

### Issue: Payment failed on Chapa

**Meaning**: Your payment was declined or cancelled.

**Solution**:
1. Check your card details
2. Ensure you have sufficient balance
3. Try again with a different payment method
4. Contact your bank if issue persists

## FAQ

### Q: How long do credits last?
**A**: Credits never expire. You can use them anytime.

### Q: Can I get a refund?
**A**: Yes, contact Chapa support for refund requests.

### Q: What if I don't use all my credits?
**A**: They stay in your wallet. You can use them for future interviews.

### Q: Can I transfer credits to another user?
**A**: No, credits are personal to your account.

### Q: What if I fail an interview?
**A**: The credit is still deducted. You can try again with another job.

### Q: Can I get free credits?
**A**: Check for promotional offers or contact support.

### Q: How do I know if my payment was successful?
**A**: You'll see "Payment Confirmed!" message and your wallet balance will update.

### Q: What if my payment was charged but credits weren't added?
**A**: Contact support with your transaction reference. We'll investigate.
