2. Employer Reviews Application
   └─> Shortlists Candidate

   ├─> Anti-Cheat Initialized
   ├─> Identity Verification Started

   ├─> Answer Recorded
   ├─> Anti-Cheat Monitoring Active

   ├─> AI Evaluates Responses

   ├─> Creates Report

   ├─> Views Interview Report
